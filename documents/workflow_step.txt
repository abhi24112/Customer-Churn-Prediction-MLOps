NOTE: 0.80 F1 is not achievable on this dataset, even with duration.
The UCI bank dataset has a known published benchmark ceiling. Top Kaggle solutions with full feature engineering, ensembles, and SMOTE land at 0.68–0.72 F1. Nobody breaks 0.75 cleanly on this data.
Why:

11.7% positive rate is brutal
poutcome=unknown for 82% of records kills the strongest real predictor
After duration, no other feature has strong discriminative power
The remaining features (age, balance, job) have massive overlap between yes/no classes

What would realistically we can get to 0.68–0.72:

Better data — if you had more historical campaign records with known poutcome, the model would improve significantly. The unknown bucket is the real ceiling.
Ensemble — stack XGBoost + LightGBM + CatBoost and use a meta-learner. Realistically adds +2–4% F1.
Calibrated threshold per segment — different thresholds for poutcome=unknown vs known records.
